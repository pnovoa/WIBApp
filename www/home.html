Welcome to WIBApp! This is a software tool for a weightless, interval-based approach to multicriteria decision making.

WIBApp automates the selection of alternatives in multi-criteria decision problems. These tasks arise in scenarios such as the (a posteriori) evaluation of optimal or high-quality solutions to an optimization problem.

The tool requires simple input data and provides a comprehensive evaluation of alternatives to assist decision makers in selecting the most suitable ones. More details are available in the Help section.
